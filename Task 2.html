Welcome to PizzaBot

Your Friendly Pizza Ordering Assistant

Purpose and Scope

Purpose: The pizza ordering chatbot is designed to streamline the process of ordering pizzas for customers of a pizza delivery service.

Scope: The chatbot's primary scope revolves around assisting customers in placing pizza orders. It should handle tasks related to menu exploration, order customization, order placement, and basic customer queries.

Target Audience

  • Pizza Lovers: Individuals who enjoy ordering pizzas for themselves, family, or friends.
  • Tech-Savvy Users: Customers comfortable with using online interfaces and chatbots for ordering.

Specific Tasks

  • Menu Exploration: Browse the pizza menu with options, prices, and descriptions. Get details about available toppings, crusts, and sizes.
  • Order Customization: Personalize your pizza by selecting toppings, crust types, and sizes. Discover popular combinations or specialty pizzas.
  • Order Placement: Provide delivery details: address, contact info, and preferred delivery time. Review order before confirming.
